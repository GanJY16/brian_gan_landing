About Brian

20 Years of Cross-Industry Expertise

Brian Gan is a seasoned trainer and coach with over 15 years dedicated to helping organisations build cultures of efficiency, effectiveness, and continuous improvement. He holds a Bachelor of Industrial Engineering (Honours) from Australia and brings deep practical knowledge from over 20 years working across manufacturing, financial shared services, property development, healthcare, and warehousing.

What sets Brian apart is his rare ability to bridge theory and practice. He has personally led Lean transformations on the shop floor — not just facilitated workshops — giving him the credibility and stories that make complex concepts click for participants at every level, from operators to senior managers.

He has conducted more than 46 public and in-house training sessions, representing about 100 client organisations across manufacturing, trading, and shared services industries.
